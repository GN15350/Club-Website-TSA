Start a New Club

Want to Start a New Club but Don’t Where to Start?

Starting something new can seem daunting, but we’ve broken down the process into 6 easy steps.

1. Pre-Approval

To get started, you‘ll need to get pre-approval through Mr. Addo, our campus club coordinator. make sure to create and link a pitch about your club, and then you can schedule a meeting through the google sheet.

2. Find A Monitor

After you get approval, you’ll need to find a employee monitor. Their job only includes monitoring the club, not to participate or sponsor. When you find one, you’ll both need to complete the acknowledgment form.

3. FNAB (Local) Form

Next, contact Mr. Addo that you have found a monitor and he’ll send you the FNAB (LOCAL) acknowledgment Form. You’ll have to fill it out and then send it to your monitor, who will need to upload the form to Laserfiche Student Groups.

4. Pre-Approval

Now you’ll have to wait for the district to approve of your club. This may take 2-3 weeks, but the waiting time will vary depending on how many requests the district is receiving. When they finally approve, Mr. Addo will send a confirmation to you and your monitor.

5. Waiting

We know, it’s boring and feels frustrating but the district is requesting other student’s requests as well. Unfortunately, this means it might take about 2–3 weeks. Once it’s approved, Mr. Addo will send a confirmation to you and your monitor.

6. Start Meeting!

Once you’ve received confirmation, you may began all club activities. Make sure to have permission slips for club members that wish to join. You can find a downloadable version of the slip here.
