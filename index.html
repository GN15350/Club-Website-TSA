Our Story

First opening in 1902, Frisco High School is known for its spirit and being “the original” Frisco high school. We foster relationships founded on service and integrity, inspire intellectual creativity, respect tradition, and cultivate lifelong learners who embrace diversity and challenges in a global society.

DON’T KNOW WHAT CLUB TO JOIN?

Here are some of the most common types of clubs we offer at our school. Find a category that resonates with you? Just click the “View Clubs” button to find a club to join.

Academic

Academic clubs bring together those who are intellectually curious and ready to push themselves outside of their school curriculum. These clubs make learning engaging through hands-on activities, out-of-school competitions, and foster meaningful relationships with peers.
